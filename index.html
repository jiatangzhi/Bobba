Especially Salted Egg Yolk Milk Tea with Cheese Foam



Bubble tea, pearl milk tea, bubble milk tea, tapioca milk tea, boba tea, or boba is a tea-based drink accompanied by chewy tapioca balls("boba" or "pearls"), but it can be made with other toppings as well, such as grass jelly, aloe vera, red bean, or popping boba. It has many varieties and flavors, but the two most popular varieties are pearl black milk tea and pearl green milk tea ("pearl" signifies the tapioca balls at the bottom). It is possible to adjust the level of sugar and ice. Enjoy!
